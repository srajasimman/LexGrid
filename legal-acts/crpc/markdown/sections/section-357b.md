# Section 357B — Compensation to be in addition to fine under Section 326A or Section 376D of Indian Penal Code 1

**Act:** Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973  
**Chapter:** 27 — The Judgement

---

The compensation payable by the State Government under section 357A shall be in addition to the payment of fine to the victim under section 326A, 376AB, section 376D, 376DA, 376DB of the Indian Penal Code 2 .
