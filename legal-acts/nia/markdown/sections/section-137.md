# Section 137 — Presumption as to foreign law

**Act:** National Investigation Agency Act, 2008  
**Chapter:** 16 — Of International Law

---

The law of any foreign country regarding promissory notes, bills of exchange and cheques shall 
 be presumed to be the same as that of India, unless and until the contrary is proved.
