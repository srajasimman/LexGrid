# Section 25 — When day of maturity is a holdiay

**Act:** National Investigation Agency Act, 2008  
**Chapter:** 02 — Of Notes, Bills and Cheques

---

When the day on which a promissory note or bill of exchange is at maturity is a public holiday, the instrument shall be deemed to be due on the next preceding business day. Explanations 
 The expression “public holiday” includes Sundays and any other day declared by the Central Government, by notification in the Official Gazette, to be a public holiday.
