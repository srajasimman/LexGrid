# Section 121 — Effect of rules in First Schedule

**Act:** Code of Civil Procedure, 1908  
**Chapter:** 10 — Part X – Rules

---

The rules in a First Schedule shall have effect as if enacted in the body of this Code until annulted or altered in accordance with the provisions of this Part.
