# Section 35 — Costs

**Act:** Code of Civil Procedure, 1908  
**Chapter:** 01 — Part I – Suits in General

---

Subject to such conditions and limitations as may be prescribed, and to the provisions of law for the time being in force, the costs of and incident to all suits shall be in the discretion of the Court, and the Court shall have full power to determine by whom or out of what property and to what extent such costs are to be paid, and to give all necessary directions for the purposes aforesaid. 
 The fact that the Court has no jurisdiction to try the suit shall be no bar to the exercise of such powers. 
 Where the Court directs that any costs shall not follow the event, the Court shall state its reasons in writing.
