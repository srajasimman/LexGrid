# Section 17 — Suits for immovable property situate within jurisdiction of different Courts

**Act:** Code of Civil Procedure, 1908  
**Chapter:** 01 — Part I – Suits in General

---

Where a suit is to obtain relief respecting, or compensation for wrong to, immovable property situate within the jurisdiction of different Court, the suit my be instituted in any Court within the local limits of whose jurisdiction any portion of the property is situate; 
 Provided that, in respect of the value of the subject matter of the suit, the entire claim is cognizable by such Court.
