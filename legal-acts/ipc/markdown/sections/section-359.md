# Section 359 — Kidnapping

**Act:** Indian Penal Code, 1860  
**Chapter:** 16 — Of Offences Affecting Life

---

Kidnapping is of two kinds: 
 kidnapping from India, and 
 kidnapping from lawful guardianship.
