# Chapter 06 — Of Offences Against the State

**Act:** Indian Penal Code, 1860  
**Sections:** 12

---

## Sections

- [Section 121](../sections/section-121.md) — Waging, or attempting to wage war, or abetting waging of war, against the Government of India
- [Section 121A](../sections/section-121a.md) — Conspiracy to commit offences punishable by section 121
- [Section 122](../sections/section-122.md) — Collecting arms, etc., with intention of waging war against the Government of India
- [Section 123](../sections/section-123.md) — Concealing with intent to facilitate design to wage war
- [Section 124](../sections/section-124.md) — Assaulting President, Governor, etc., with intent to compel or restrain the exercise of any lawful power
- [Section 124A](../sections/section-124a.md) — Sedition
- [Section 125](../sections/section-125.md) — Waging war against any Asiatic Power in alliance with the Government of India
- [Section 126](../sections/section-126.md) — Committing depredation on territories of Power at peace with the Government of India
- [Section 127](../sections/section-127.md) — Receiving property taken by war or depredation mentioned in sections 125 and 126
- [Section 128](../sections/section-128.md) — Public servant voluntarily allowing prisoner of state or war to escape
- [Section 129](../sections/section-129.md) — Public servant negligently suffering such prisoner to escape
- [Section 130](../sections/section-130.md) — Aiding escape of, rescuing or harbouring such prisoner
