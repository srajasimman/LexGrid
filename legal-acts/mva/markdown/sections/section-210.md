# Section 210 — Courts to send intimation about conviction

**Act:** Motor Vehicles Act, 1988  
**Chapter:** 13 — Offences, Penalties and Procedure

---

Every Court by which any person holding a driving license is convicted of an offence under this Act or of an offence in the commission of which a motor vehicle was used, shall send intimation to— 
 the licensing authority which issued the driving license, and 
 the licensing authority by whom the license was last renewed, and every such intimation shall state the name and address of the holder of the license, the license number, the date of issue and renewal of the same, the nature of the offence, the punishment awarded for the same and such other particulars as may be prescribed.
