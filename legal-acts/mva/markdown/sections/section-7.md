# Section 7 — Restrictions on the granting of learner’s licenses for certain vehicles

**Act:** Motor Vehicles Act, 1988  
**Chapter:** 02 — Licensing of Drivers of Motor Vehicles

---

No person shall be granted a learner’s license to drive a transport vehicle unless he has held a driving license to drive a light motor vehicle for at least one year. 
 No person under the age of eighteen years shall be granted a learner’s license to drive a motor cycle without gear except with the consent in writing of the person having the care of the person desiring the learner’s license.
