# Section 137 — Power of Central Government to make rules

**Act:** Motor Vehicles Act, 1988  
**Chapter:** 08 — Control of Traffic

---

The Central Government may make rules to provide for all or any of the following matters, namely— 
 the occasions on which signals shall be made by drivers of motor vehicles and such signals under section 121; 
 the manner in which the licenses and certificates may be produced to the police officer under section 130.
