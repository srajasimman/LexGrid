# Section 26 — Maintenance of State Registers of Driving Licences

**Act:** Motor Vehicles Act, 1988  
**Chapter:** 02 — Licensing of Drivers of Motor Vehicles

---

Each State Government shall maintain, in such form as may be prescribed by the Central Government, a register to be known as the State Register of Driving Licences, in respect of driving licenses issued and renewed by the licensing authorities of the State Government, containing the following particulars, namely— 
 names and addresses of holders of driving licenses; 
 license numbers; 
 dates of issue or renewal of licenses; 
 dates of expiry of licenses; 
 classes and types of vehicles authorised to be driven; and 
 such other particulars as the Central Government may prescribe. 
 Each State Government shall supply to the Central Government a printed copy or copy in such other form as the Central Government may require, of the State Register of Driving Licences and shall inform the Central Government without delay of all additions to and other amendments in such register made from time to time. 
 The State Register of Driving Licences shall be maintained in such manner as may be prescribed by the State Government.
