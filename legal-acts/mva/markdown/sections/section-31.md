# Section 31 — Disqualifications for the grant of conductor’s license

**Act:** Motor Vehicles Act, 1988  
**Chapter:** 03 — Licensing of Conductors of Stage Carriages

---

No person under the age of eighteen years shall hold, or be granted, a conductor’s license. 
 The licensing authority may refuse to issue a conductor’s license— 
 if the applicant does not possess the minimum educational qualification; 
 if the medical certificate produced by the applicant discloses that he is physically unfit to act as a conductor; and 
 if any previous conductor’s license held by the applicant was revoked.
