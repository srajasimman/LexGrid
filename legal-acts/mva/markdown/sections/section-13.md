# Section 13 — Extent of effectiveness of licenses, to drive motor vehicles

**Act:** Motor Vehicles Act, 1988  
**Chapter:** 02 — Licensing of Drivers of Motor Vehicles

---

A learner’s license or a driving license issued under this Act shall be effective throughout India.
