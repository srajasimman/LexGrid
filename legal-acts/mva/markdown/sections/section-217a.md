# Section 217A — Renewal of permits, driving licenses and registration granted under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1939

**Act:** Motor Vehicles Act, 1988  
**Chapter:** 14 — Miscellaneous

---

Notwithstanding the repeal by sub-section (1) of section 217 of the enactments referred to in that sub-section, any certificate of fitness or registration or license or permit issued or granted under the said enactments may be renewed under this Act.
