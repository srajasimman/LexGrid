# Section 31 — Admissions not conclusive proof, but may estop

**Act:** Indian Evidence Act, 1872  
**Chapter:** 02 — Of the Relevancy of Facts

---

Admissions are not conclusive proof of the matters admitted but they may operate as estoppels under the provisions hereinafter contained. Sections 32 to 33 – Statements by persons who cannot be called as witnesses
