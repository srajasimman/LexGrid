# Section 51 — Grounds of opinion, when relevant

**Act:** Indian Evidence Act, 1872  
**Chapter:** 02 — Of the Relevancy of Facts

---

Whenever the opinion of any living person is relevant, the grounds on which such opinion is based are also relevant. 
 Illustrations 
 An expert may give an account of experiments performed by him for the purpose of forming his opinion. Sections 52 to 55 – Character when relevant
