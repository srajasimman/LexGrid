# Chapter 18 — Of Offences Relating To Documents And To Property Marks

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Sections:** 16

---

## Sections

- [Section 335](../sections/section-335.md) — Making a false document.
- [Section 336](../sections/section-336.md) — Forgery.
- [Section 337](../sections/section-337.md) — Forgery of record of Court or of public register, etc.
- [Section 338](../sections/section-338.md) — Forgery of valuable security, will, etc.
- [Section 339](../sections/section-339.md) — Having possession of document described in section 337 or 338, knowing it to be forged and intending to use it as genuine.
- [Section 340](../sections/section-340.md) — Forged document or electronic record and using it as genuine.
- [Section 341](../sections/section-341.md) — Making or possessing counterfeit seal, etc., with intent to commit forgery punishable under section 338.
- [Section 342](../sections/section-342.md) — Counterfeiting device or mark used for authenticating documents described in section 338, or possessing counterfeit marked material.
- [Section 343](../sections/section-343.md) — Fraudulent cancellation, destruction, etc., of will, authority to adopt, or valuable security.
- [Section 344](../sections/section-344.md) — Falsification of accounts.
- [Section 345](../sections/section-345.md) — Property mark.
- [Section 346](../sections/section-346.md) — Tampering with property mark with intent to cause injury.
- [Section 347](../sections/section-347.md) — Counterfeiting a property mark.
- [Section 348](../sections/section-348.md) — Making or possession of any instrument for counterfeiting a property mark.
- [Section 349](../sections/section-349.md) — Selling goods marked with a counterfeit property mark.
- [Section 350](../sections/section-350.md) — Making a false mark upon any receptacle containing goods.
