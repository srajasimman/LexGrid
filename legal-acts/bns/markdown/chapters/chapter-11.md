# Chapter 11 — Of Offences Against The Public Tranquillity

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Sections:** 9

---

## Sections

- [Section 189](../sections/section-189.md) — Unlawful assembly.
- [Section 190](../sections/section-190.md) — Every member of unlawful assembly guilty of offence committed in prosecution of common object.
- [Section 191](../sections/section-191.md) — Rioting.
- [Section 192](../sections/section-192.md) — Wantonly giving provocation with intent to cause riot- if rioting be committed; if not committed.
- [Section 193](../sections/section-193.md) — Liability of owner, occupier etc., of land on which an unlawful assembly or riot takes place.
- [Section 194](../sections/section-194.md) — Affray.
- [Section 195](../sections/section-195.md) — Assaulting or obstructing public servant when suppressing riot, etc.
- [Section 196](../sections/section-196.md) — Promoting enmity between different groups on ground of religion, race, place of birth, residence, language, etc., and doing acts prejudicial to maintenance of harmony.
- [Section 197](../sections/section-197.md) — Imputations, assertions prejudicial to national integration.
