# Chapter 07 — Of Offences Against The State

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Sections:** 12

---

## Sections

- [Section 147](../sections/section-147.md) — Waging, or attempting to wage war, or abetting waging of war, against the Government of India.
- [Section 148](../sections/section-148.md) — Conspiracy to commit offences punishable by section 147.
- [Section 149](../sections/section-149.md) — Collecting arms, etc., with intention of waging war against the Government of India.
- [Section 150](../sections/section-150.md) — Concealing with intent to facilitate design to wage war.
- [Section 151](../sections/section-151.md) — Assaulting President, Governor, etc., with intent to compel or restrain the exercise of any lawful power.
- [Section 152](../sections/section-152.md) — Acts endangering sovereignty unity and integrity of India.
- [Section 153](../sections/section-153.md) — Waging war against Government of any foreign State at peace with the Government of India.
- [Section 154](../sections/section-154.md) — Committing depredation on territories of foreign State at peace with the Government of India.
- [Section 155](../sections/section-155.md) — Receiving property taken by war or depredation mentioned in sections 153 and 154.
- [Section 156](../sections/section-156.md) — Public servant voluntarily allowing prisoner of state or war to escape.
- [Section 157](../sections/section-157.md) — Public servant negligently suffering such prisoner to escape.
- [Section 158](../sections/section-158.md) — Aiding escape of, rescuing or harbouring such prisoner.
