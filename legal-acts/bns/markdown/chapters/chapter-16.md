**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Sections:** 5

---

## Sections

- [Section 300](../sections/section-300.md) — Disturbing religious assembly.
- [Section 301](../sections/section-301.md) — Trespassing on burial places, etc.
