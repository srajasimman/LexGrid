# Section 40 — Commencement and continuance of the right of private defence of the body.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 03 — General Exceptions

---

IPC [Section 102 
 The](../sections/section-102.md) right of private defence of the body commences as soon as a reasonable apprehension of danger to the body arises from an attempt or threat to commit the offence though the offence may not have been committed; and it continues as long as such apprehension of danger to the body continues.
