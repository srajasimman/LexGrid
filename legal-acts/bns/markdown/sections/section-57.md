# Section 57 — Abetting commission of offence by the public or by more than ten persons.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 04 — Of Abetment, Criminal Conspiracy And Attempt

---

IPC [Section 117 
 Whoever](../sections/section-117.md) abets the commission of an offence by the public generally or by any number or class of persons exceeding ten, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years and with fine. 
 Illustration: A affixes in a public place a placard instigating a sect consisting of more than ten members to meet at a certain time and place, for the purpose of attacking the members of an adverse sect, while engaged in a procession. A has committed the offence defined in this section.
