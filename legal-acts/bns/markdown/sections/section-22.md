# Section 22 — Act of a person of unsound mind.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 03 — General Exceptions

---

IPC [Section 84 
 Nothing](../sections/section-84.md) is an offence which is done by a person who, at the time of doing it, by reason of mental illness, is incapable of knowing the nature of the act, or that he is doing what is either wrong or contrary to law.
