# Section 14 — Act done by a person bound, or by mistake of fact believing himself bound, by law.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 03 — General Exceptions

---

IPC [Section 76 
 Nothing](../sections/section-76.md) is an offence which is done by a person who is, or who by reason of a mistake of fact and not by reason of a mistake of law in good faith believes himself to be, bound by law to do it. 
 Illustrations: 
 A, a soldier, fires on a mob by the order of his superior officer, in conformity with the commands of the law. A has committed no offence. 
 A, an officer of a Court, being ordered by that Court to arrest Y, and after due enquiry, believing Z to be Y, arrests Z. A has committed no offence.
