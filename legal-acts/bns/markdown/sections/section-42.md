# Section 42 — When such right extends to causing any harm other than death.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 03 — General Exceptions

---

IPC [Section 104 
 If](../sections/section-104.md) the offence, the committing of which, or the attempting to commit which occasions the exercise of the right of private defence, be theft, mischief, or criminal trespass, not of any of the descriptions specified in section 41 , that right does not extend to the voluntary causing of death, but does extend, subject to the restrictions specified in section 37 , to the voluntary causing to the wrong-doer of any harm other than death.
