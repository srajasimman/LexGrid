# Section 39 — When such right extends to causing any harm other than death.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 03 — General Exceptions

---

IPC [Section 101 
 If](../sections/section-101.md) the offence be not of any of the descriptions specfied in section 38 , the right of private defence of the body does not extend to the voluntary causing of death to the assailant, but does extend, under the restrictions specified in section 37 , to the voluntary causing to the assailant of any harm other than death.
