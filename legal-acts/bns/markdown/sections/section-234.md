# Section 234 — Issuing or signing false certificate.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 14 — Of False Evidence And Offences Against Public Justice

---

Whoever issues or signs any certificate required by law to be given or signed, or relating to any fact of which such certificate is by law admissible in evidence, knowing or believing that such certificate is false in any material point, shall be punished in the same manner as if he gave false evidence. 
 BNSS Classification 
 The same as for giving false evidence. 
 Non-cognizable 
 Bailable 
 Triable by Court by which offence of giving false evidence is triable.
