# Section 114 — Hurt.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 06 — Of Offences Affecting The Human Body

---

Whoever causes bodily pain, disease or infirmity to any person is said to cause hurt.
