# Section 34 — Things done in private defence.

**Act:** Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023  
**Chapter:** 03 — General Exceptions

---

IPC [Section 96 
 Nothing](../sections/section-96.md) is an offence which is done in the exercise of the right of private defence.
